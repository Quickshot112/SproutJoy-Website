PROGRAMMES OPTIONS

Play House

16–36 months old

The Play House programme offers a gentle introduction to early learning, designed specifically for toddlers in their most curious and exploratory stage. With a focus on sensory exploration and motor development, children engage in age-appropriate play that encourages touch, movement and sound. From interactive games to soft physical play, our nurturing environment supports social bonding, early language development and emotional security.

Key Features: Daily sensory bins for tactile learning, animal sound games for early language development, soft climbing mats for motor skills, bubble play for sensory stimulation, guided story time or music exploration, and dedicated nap and wind-down routines. Children also enjoy breakfast and snack breaks, indoor/outdoor free play, and a 1:4 staff-to-child ratio to ensure safety and personalized attention.

Operation Time: Monday to Friday, 7:30 AM – 5:30 PM

Booking Requirement: Pre-registration required 2 days before desired start date.

Price: RM800/month (includes snacks)

Play School

3–4 Years old

Our Play School programme provides a more structured play-based environment where young learners begin to express themselves, form friendships and explore the world through imagination. Through creative storytelling, dress-up, hands-on crafts and guided play, children build key skills in communication, problem-solving, and self-regulation.

Key Features: Themed dress-up corner for imaginative play, playdough and puzzle stations for fine motor skills, puppet-led story time to build early literacy, group circle time with movement games, and daily sensory or art activities to inspire creativity. Afternoon sessions include dramatic play, music exploration, and rest time for full-day children. 1:5 staff-to-child ratio ensures personalized support and supervision.

Operation Time: Monday to Friday, 7:30 AM – 5:30 PM

Booking Requirement: Must book at least 3 days in advance. Priority given to returning students.

Price: RM850/month (art materials included)

Junior Whaley

5 Years old

Junior Whaley is designed to expand curiosity, independence, and foundational academic thinking. Children explore big ideas through fun, age-appropriate experiences like science discovery, movement games, music and art. With a balance of structured learning and creative freedom, this programme fosters cognitive development, social-emotional growth, and confidence in expressing ideas.

Key Features: Morning yoga movement games, music & instrument play, hands-on science experiments, creative art and craft sessions, story circle reflections, quiet rest periods, outdoor gross motor activities, and puzzle-based learning. 1:6 staff-to-child ratio.

Operation Time: Monday to Friday, 7:30 AM – 5:30 PM

Booking Requirement: Registration required at least 3 days in advance. Subject to availability.

Price: RM900/month (materials included)

Senior Whaley

6 Years old

Senior Whaley is where future-ready learners thrive. This programme focuses on preparing children for primary school through critical thinking challenges, project-based exploration and teamwork. Whether building with recycled materials, solving math puzzles or leading a group scavenger hunt, children learn to plan, collaborate, and problem-solve.

Key Features: Math Magic Zone for critical thinking, hands-on robotics building, group Recycled City eco-projects, indoor/outdoor scavenger hunts, daily reflection & journaling sessions, challenge board games for teamwork, and free project time for self-guided exploration. 1:7 staff-to-child ratio.

Operation Time: Monday to Friday, 7:30 AM – 5:30 PM

Booking Requirement: Registration required at least 1 week in advance. Open for year-round intake.

Price: RM950/month (includes workbook and activity kit)
