Pop-up nanny

Our pop-up nanny service offers on-demand care tailored for events, emergencies, or spontaneous needs. Whether it's a work meeting or a special night out, we've got you covered with short-notice support.

Key Features: Flexible hourly or daily booking, trained nannies for events (weddings, parties, conferences), emergency childcare, engaging play sessions, meal and nap supervision, 1:3 staff-to-child ratio.

Our experienced nannies are vetted and trained to adapt to new environments quickly, ensuring your child is safe, comfortable, and engaged even on short notice.

Operation Time: Available daily from 7:00 AM – 10:00 PM (extended hours upon request)

Booking Requirement: Minimum 4-hour notice required for same-day bookings

Price: RM50/hour or RM400/day (discounts for multiple children)
