OUR SERVICES

DAYCARE PROGRAMMES

Our daycare offers thoughtfully designed programmes for children aged 16 months to 6 years, supporting growth through play and exploration. From sensory play in Play House to school readiness in Senior Whaley, each stage encourages creativity, social skills, and confidence in a safe, nurturing environment.

NANNY CARE PLAN

The Nanny Care Plan offers tailored childcare services based on your child’s age and needs. It includes an Infant Plan for newborns (0–2 years) with specialized care, a Toddler Plan (2–4 years) focused on interactive learning and play, and a Full-Day Plan (4+ years) for comprehensive, all-day nanny support. Each plan ensures safe, age-appropriate care in a nurturing environment.

POP-UP NANNY

Our Pop-Up Nanny service offers flexible, on-site childcare for special events, short-term needs, or weekend programmes. Trained caregivers create a safe and engaging play environment, allowing parents to focus while their children are well cared for nearby. It’s the perfect solution for temporary or event-based childcare support.
