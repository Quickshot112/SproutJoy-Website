ORGANIZATION'S BACKGROUND

Founding Story

SproutJoy was founded in 2021 by Mr. Nuaim, who noticed how many working parents in his community struggled to find a daycare they could truly trust and a place that felt like a second home for their little ones. As someone passionate about early childhood education, Mr. Nuaim envisioned a daycare that would be more than just a place to leave children during work hours. He wanted to create a nurturing environment where every child felt safe, loved, and free to discover their strengths and creativity. Starting with just a small group of children in a cozy classroom, SproutJoy quickly became known for its warm atmosphere and dedicated team. Today, SproutJoy continues to grow as a trusted daycare Centre that stays true to its original mission: providing “Safe Hands, Happy Hearts” for every child and peace of mind for every parent.

Purpose

At SproutJoy, our purpose is to create a safe and joyful environment where every child feels loved, valued, and confident to explore the world around them. We believe that early childhood is a precious time for growth and discovery, and we are here to nurture each child’s unique potential through playful learning, care, and guidance. We aim to support parents by providing trustworthy, high-quality childcare that gives them peace of mind, knowing their little ones are in good hands each day.

Heartfelt Closing

Thank you for taking the time to get to know SproutJoy. We truly believe that every child deserves a place where they feel safe, loved, and happy each day. It is our greatest joy to watch your little ones grow, learn, and discover their strengths in an environment that feels like a second home. We look forward to welcoming you and your child into our SproutJoy family, where safe hands and happy hearts are at the heart of everything we do.
